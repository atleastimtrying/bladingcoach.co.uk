About

Blading Coach is a venture from Anders Fisher, intending to help people learn to skate(rollerblade) and learn to skate better!

Anders has been skating in some form or other since 1996 and has a dedicated methodical approach to improving his and your skating. His background is aggressive skating and helping others improve their technique, body positioning and confidence in their tricks.
